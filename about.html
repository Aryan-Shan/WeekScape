WeekScape

Welcome to WeekScape, a personalized digital companion on your life journey. This minimalist platform helps you embrace the passage of time and stay motivated by visualizing your life in weeks. Register with your name, birthdate, and expected lifespan, and witness the transformation of your life into a collection of squares representing weeks. Each filled square represents a week lived, while the empty ones symbolize the time ahead. With an elegant design and a touch of inspiration from random quotes, WeekScape serves as a gentle reminder that your time here on this small planet is limited so use it wisely.
